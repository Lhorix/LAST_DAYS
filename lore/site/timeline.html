Timeline

Too many people no longer remember what year it is according to the Gregorian calendar of the Old World. Now another time system is being used, once created by Creeper in March 16th 13 A.E. during the moment of his greatest madness. It is based on "The Event" when the Moon, or the Belly, was blown up due to a failed portal experiment.

However, those who managed to restore the chronology on the basis of old records, including O.I. reports, unanimously declare: the Event occurred in 1976 A.D.
